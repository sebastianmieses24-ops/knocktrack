Star Rating Standards
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Elite Closer
Close rate above 15%. Consistently high volume with strong results. This rep is a top performer — mentor material. Focus: Keep them motivated, give them leadership opportunities and territory expansion.
⭐⭐⭐⭐
Strong Performer
Close rate 8–15%. Good numbers, solid work ethic. May have occasional inconsistency but generally reliable. Focus: Fine-tune objection handling and push for consistency every single day.
⭐⭐⭐
Developing Rep
Close rate 3–8%. Shows potential but needs more reps. Likely inconsistent — good days and bad days. Focus: Closing training, shadowing top reps, accountability check-ins.
⭐⭐
Needs Improvement
Close rate 1–3% or very low door count. Either struggling to close or not knocking enough. Focus: Evaluate if the issue is activity or skill. Assign a mentor, set minimum daily door targets.
⭐
At Risk
Close rate under 1% or minimal activity. This rep is either not putting in the work or not absorbing training. Focus: Immediate intervention — performance improvement plan or reassignment.
📌 Law of Averages Reminder
A rep who knocks 500 doors and closes 1 needs closing training — their activity is there but conversion is broken. A rep who knocks 10 doors and closes 5 has elite closing skills but poor work ethic — push volume, not skill. The best reps knock 100+ doors/week AND close above 8%.
